Transportation

Gangneung city can be reached via various means of transport. Followings are a few of the recommended ways to reach our conference venue.

Airport Limousine Bus



We recommend taking an airport limousine bus to Gangneung City. This bus service is available from both Incheon Airport(both terminals) and Gimpo Airport. This will be the most convenient way to get to the conference venue as it is a direct bus from the airport to Gangneung City. Note: This is the most convenient and recommended mode of transportation.

Gangneung

Gangneung is a city in the province of Gangwon-do, on the east coast of South Korea. Gangneung is the largest costal city of South Korea and has many tourist attractions, such as Jeongdongjin, a very popular area for watching the sun rise, and Gyeongpo Beach.

Travelling around the city and Korea.

You can travel around the city by taking public buses or taking a taxi. Kakao Map or Naver Maps can be used to explore the city and are availiable in english.
